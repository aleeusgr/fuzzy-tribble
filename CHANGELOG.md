# Changelog - Archive Codex Project

## [Unreleased] - 2025-11-03

### Added

- **Project Vision & Mission Framework**: Established core mission to preserve science, mathematics, and systematic thought captured in computer code. Defined organizational pivot from Unitarian Universalist Association of Technologists to preservation-focused initiative inspired by Foundation series and Anna's Archive. Articulated five core principles derived from UU values: Universal Access, Worth and Dignity, Justice and Equity, Interdependence, and Free and Responsible Search.

- **Developer Relations & Community Strategy**: Developed comprehensive social media engagement plan across X/Twitter (primary), LinkedIn (secondary), Substack (weekly newsletter), and internet forums. Created content pillar framework: Rescue Stories (40%), The Crisis (25%), Community Spotlights (20%), Educational Content (10%), Movement Building (5%). Defined platform-specific tactics, voice guidelines, and 90-day launch campaign structure.

- **MVP Specification - The Resurrection Drive**: Designed bootable USB drive architecture (256-512GB) containing self-contained NixOS environment with offline nixpkgs repository, curated scientific/mathematical software collections ("Essential 100" expanding to 500+), comprehensive documentation database, and user-friendly interface. System enables survivors to boot any x86_64 laptop, access preserved computational knowledge, build software from source, and rebuild information infrastructure without network connectivity. Implementation roadmap spans 24 weeks across three phases: Proof of Concept, MVP v1.0, and Refinement.
